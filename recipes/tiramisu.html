Step by step tiramisu

  1. Prepare the mascarpone mixture in large mixture whisk 6 egg yolks and sugar until it becomes thick and pale
  2. Add mascarpone cheese to the egg mixture and mix until well combines
  3. In separe bowl, whip the heavy cream
  4. Combine the heavy cream with mascarpone
  5. Quickly dip each ladyfingers cookie into the espresso
  6. Get a baking dish and arrange the dipped ladyfingers ina single layer at the bottom
  7. Spread half of the mascarpone mixture onto ladyfingers
  8. Put another layer of ladyfingers and spread mascarpone again
  9. Cover the dish with plastic wrap and put it into fridge for 4 hours
  10. Use the cocoa powder to cover the top of the tiramisu
  11. SERVE AND ENJOY THE SWEETNESS
