Step by step new york styled pizza

Before you start making the actual pizza

  1. Preheat the oven to the highest temperature possible
  2. Take your dough out of your fridge and let it run to your room temperature

Lets finally make it!

  1. Mix together tomato sauce, minced garlic, dried oregano, dried basil, salt and pepper in a small bowl
  2. Shred the mozzarela cheese
  3. Sprinkle on pizza peel so it wont stick to it
  4. On a lightly floured surface, gently stretch or roll out the pizza dough into a circle or rectangle
  5. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving small area around the edges
  6. Sprinkle the cheese over the sauce
  7. Place the pizza onto a baking sheet and put it into preheated oven
  8. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and cheese is bubbly and melted
  9. Once out, sprinkle grated parmesan cheese over the pizza and dont forget to do the same with the olive oil
  10. Slice, serve and its PIZZA TIME!
